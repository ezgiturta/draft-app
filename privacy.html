Privacy Policy

Draft — How to Become a Writer. Last updated: April 23, 2026.

This policy describes what information Draft ("the app", "we") collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user. By using Draft you agree to the practices described here.

What we collect

  • Account info: your email address when you sign in, used only to restore your account and progress on other devices.
  • Writing content: the one-sentence pitch you write and any notes or drafts you save stay on your device. When synced for backup, they are stored encrypted in our database (Supabase) tied to your account.
  • Usage data: streak count, session timestamps, and which daily rings you complete. Used to power the streak feature and personalize prompts.
  • Subscription status: whether you have an active subscription, handled by RevenueCat. Apple processes all payments — we never see your card.
  • Device info: an anonymous device identifier used for fraud detection on free trials.

What we do NOT collect

  • No location data.
  • No contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access.
  • No advertising identifiers. Draft has no ads.
  • No third-party analytics that track you across apps.

How we use it

  • To run the app: save your progress, deliver daily writing prompts, manage your subscription.
  • To send you daily reminders via local push notifications (you can disable in iOS Settings).
  • To improve the app: diagnose crashes and fix bugs using Apple's built-in crash reporting.

AI processing

Your one-sentence pitch is sent to Anthropic's API to generate personalized daily writing prompts. Anthropic does not train models on this data. The pitch is not shared beyond what is necessary to produce a prompt.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your data. We share minimally with:

  • Supabase — for secure storage of your account and pitch.
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management.
  • Anthropic — for AI-generated writing prompts.
  • Apple — for payment processing and app delivery.

Children

Draft is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings, or by emailing us. Deletion is permanent.

Data retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. After account deletion, data is removed from our systems within 30 days.

Changes

If we change this policy materially, we will update the date above and notify you in the app.
